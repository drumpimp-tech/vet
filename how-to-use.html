CONTENT TYPES & SCORING Text Claim Balanced scoring across Truth, Factual, and Source Trust equally. News Headline Source Trust weighted heavily. Unverified accounts posting news claims score lower. Video Claim Truth Score weighted heavily. Skeptical of dramatic or unverifiable video claims. Image / AI Analyzes for AI generation artifacts — hands, text, lighting, backgrounds. Returns "AI Generated" verdict if likely synthetic. UNDERSTANDING SCORES 75–100 Strong — well-supported, accurate, credible source. 50–74 Moderate — some truth but possibly missing context or weak sourcing. 20–49 Low — significant accuracy or credibility issues. Treat with caution. 0–19 Critical — DANGER alert fires. Do not share. Truth How accurately the claim is stated and framed. Factual Whether the underlying facts are verifiable and correct.
