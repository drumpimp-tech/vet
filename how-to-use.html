How to Use VET

Verified Evaluated Truth — Fact Check Everything

3 Ways to VET Content
✂️

Copy & Paste

Copy any post caption or headline, paste it into the VET tab text box, tap VET This Post.

📤

Share → VET

In Instagram, TikTok, Twitter or any app — tap Share, find VET, tap it. Auto-analyzes instantly.

🌐

Browser Tab

Open a platform, scroll to a post, highlight the caption, tap VET This at the bottom.

🔗

Paste a Link

For news articles and public pages, paste the URL — VET fetches the content automatically.

💡 Best method for social media: Use the Share button inside Instagram, TikTok, or Twitter — tap Share → VET. It's one tap and fully automatic.

VET Tab
1

Select Content Type

Choose from Text Claim, News Headline, Image / AI, or Video Claim. VET adjusts its scoring criteria based on the type. For images, a photo picker opens instead of the text box.

2

Paste Your Content

Paste a post caption, headline, claim, or article URL into the text box. VET auto-detects the platform from any URL you paste.

3

Tap VET This Post

VET searches the web for live context, then runs Claude AI analysis to generate scores, flags, and sources. Results appear in seconds.

⚠️ Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and Twitter links can't be fetched automatically. For those, use Share → VET or copy the post text directly.
Browser Tab
1

Search or Pick a Platform

Type any URL or search term in the search bar at the top, or tap a platform from the grid (Instagram, Twitter/X, TikTok, Facebook, YouTube, Reddit, Truth Social, Telegram).

2

Navigate to the Post

Browse normally. Back/forward buttons are in the top left. Tap the house icon to return to the platform home screen.

3

Highlight & VET

Long-press the post caption to highlight just that text, then tap VET This at the bottom. If nothing is highlighted, VET captures the full visible page content.

Share Extension (Recommended for Social Media)
1

Open Any Post in Any App

Works in Instagram, TikTok, Twitter/X, Facebook, Safari, news apps — anywhere with a Share button.

2

Tap Share → VET

Tap the Share icon (↑), scroll the share sheet to find VET, tap it. The app opens and automatically begins analysis.

3

See Results

VET populates with the shared content, shows it to you briefly, then begins analysis. Scores and verdict appear automatically.

Content Types & Scoring
Text Claim

Balanced scoring across Truth, Factual, and Source Trust equally.

News Headline

Source Trust weighted heavily. Unverified accounts posting news claims score lower.

Video Claim

Truth Score weighted heavily. Skeptical of dramatic or unverifiable video claims.

Image / AI

Analyzes for AI generation artifacts — hands, text, lighting, backgrounds. Returns "AI Generated" verdict if likely synthetic.

Understanding Scores
75–100

Strong — well-supported, accurate, credible source.

50–74

Moderate — some truth but possibly missing context or weak sourcing.

20–49

Low — significant accuracy or credibility issues. Treat with caution.

0–19

Critical — DANGER alert fires. Do not share.

Truth

How accurately the claim is stated and framed.

Factual

Whether the underlying facts are verifiable and correct.

Source Trust

Credibility of the account or outlet that posted it.

Verdicts
✓ Verified ✓ Mostly True ⚠ Misleading ? Unverified ✗ False 🎭 Satire 🤖 AI Generated ⚠ DANGER

⚠ DANGER Alert

When any single score drops below 20, VET fires a full DANGER alert — red screen flash, haptic burst, and audio sting. A modal shows the scores and specific red flags. This content is likely harmful misinformation. Do not share it.
